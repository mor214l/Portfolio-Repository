Hi everyone and thank you for showing interest in the archery club! This club is centered around helping those new to archery and those at an intermediate level. We plan to have a walk through of the basics and have an environment for those more experienced to practice and to interact with fellow archers. If we interest you can sign up above or contact us for further questions.
Don't forget to check our calendar regularly, as we host various events and competitions throughout the month. If you're new to the sport, mark your calendars for our monthly beginner walkthrough, held on the 25th of every month. It's the perfect opportunity to receive guidance from experienced archers and learn the essential techniques. We will have equipment on site for those who want to get a feel for it.
For members already through the sign-up process and have met with the club in person we want to ensure you have gotten a feel for our on site loaner bows before visiting the equipment link and purchasing anything. The amazon list is what we recommend buying but you may purchase different brands or designs if you'd like but there are requirements. The bow must be 60", an archery glove, a quiver, and a bow case/bag.
